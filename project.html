Origin of Rice

My best food in and out of season remains jollof rice. The exact place rice originated from is still controversial.

There are different schools of thought regarding the origin of rice. Some are of the opinion that rice was one of the variants of wild grasses cultivated in Himalayas hence its ancestry is traced there.

Some others believe rice came to be through the Southern Indians, from where it spread to other Asian countries.

The converging point of the different views lies in unanimous agreement that the spread however was birthed through socialization.

Ingredients For Preparing Jollof Rice

Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Tomatoes 5 Tbsp
oil 3/4 cup oil
stock 4 cups
chicken 1kg
spice 1 Tbsp
Onions 1 medium sized

Steps For Preparing Jollof Rice

  1. Parboil the rice for 5-7mins.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pan and add onions.
  3. Add the tomatoes and allow to cook until the water is dried.
  4. Add the spices and the meat stock.
  5. To the boiling sauce,add rice.
  6. Reduce the heat and allow to cook gently.
  7. Put the food down once the water has dried up.
  8. Serve with the chicken
